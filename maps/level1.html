█ Cafe Ngā kawhē

Our ground-floor cafe offers great breakfasts, brunches, lunches, and fresh snacks. Gluten-free and vegetarian options are available. Drinks include espresso coffee, herbal teas, wine, and beer.

Our kids’ play area will entertain your little ones while you rest your feet. A parents’ room is also available.

You’ll find quality gifts and souvenirs unique to New Zealand at our ground-floor store. Choose from New Zealand–made jewellery and crafts, books by our country’s leading authors, works from Aotearoa’s finest artists, and much more.
